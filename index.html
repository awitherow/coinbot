Your cryptocurrency trading assistant.

The goal of Coinbot is one of simplicity. While hackers and bankers duke it out in attempts to do high speed microtrading, a sane approach is necessary.

Effective Risk Ratios

The biggest mistake one can make in the market is buying too soon, or selling too early. Either way, you have to sit out of the game until you get back in, usually with a cost. Coinbot lets you know when the best opportunity to buy or sell cryptocurrency is, so you don't have to stress out keeping up to date.

Secure Trading Platform

Coinbot uses the GDAX Exchange on its backend, run by Coinbase. It is one of the most well trusted and secure platform to conduct cryptocurrency trading. It offers premium coins with healthy track records. It allows users from both the United States and Europe to effectively trade Cryptocurrency.

Open Source

An Open Source codebase is the best. It allows for optimal transparency to users, provides easy in modification and community driven feature development. The Coinbot core will always remain open for the public to have input on. It is also an excellent repository to learn essential Nodejs programming skills, helping you grow as a developer.
