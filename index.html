How does it work?

Sign up for Coinbot.

First, you need to sign up for a Coinbase Account. Once you have your account and funds ready to take action, sign up for Coinbot. To sign up, your email is needed for future communication (no third party nonsense), your name to be formal, and phone number so that Coinbot can send you free of charge texts.

Relax

Let the Coinbot watch the market for you and let you know what is going on. You can relax. The Coinbot will write you if the market is

  1. accelerating upwards a good cashout,
  2. accelerating downwards to a potential buy-in,

Here, all the coinbot is doing is watching for large market fluctuations with high volume. These are the safest timeframes to make massive account gains.

Principals

Effective Risk Ratios

The biggest mistake one can make in the market is buying too soon, or selling too early. Either way, you have to sit out of the game until you get back in, usually with a cost. Coinbot lets you know when the best opportunity to buy or sell cryptocurrency is, so you don't have to stress out keeping up to date until it is relevant to you.

Secure Trading Platform

Coinbot uses the GDAX Exchange on its backend, run by Coinbase. It is one of the most well trusted and secure platform to conduct cryptocurrency trading. It offers premium coins with healthy track records. It allows users from both the United States and Europe to effectively trade Cryptocurrency.

Open Source

Interested in Programming? Welcome! An Open Source codebase is the best. It allows for optimal transparency to users, provides easy in modification and community driven feature development. The Coinbot core will always remain open for the public to have input on. It is also an excellent repository to learn essential Nodejs programming skills, helping you grow as a developer.
